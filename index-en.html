Featured Case Study

Tamar Cargo Logistics TMS

Proof of Delivery (POD) Module & Control Tower
Google AppSheet Apps Script (JS) Computer Vision SQL Logic Dashboard UX

report_problem The Challenge

The carrier faced a critical issue with reverse logistics. Delayed physical delivery receipts caused data blackouts, preventing immediate client billing. Additionally, managing a mixed fleet required collecting data from third-party drivers without exposing the company's client base or strategic routes.

psychology Solution Architecture

I developed a serverless ecosystem based on Google Workspace and GitHub Pages. The solution focuses not just on data collection, but on process intelligence:

  • Security by Segmentation: Implemented Security Filters and Slices in AppSheet. In-house drivers have authenticated access, while third parties use a secure, public "Satellite App" restricted to data submission only.
  • Rules Engine (Apps Script): Developed a JavaScript backend that instantly processes incoming images, renames files to a standard format, and dynamically organizes them in Google Drive folders by `Client > Year > Month`.
  • Web Control Tower (Advanced UX): Developed an exclusive frontend in pure JS and CSS3. The web dashboard consumes Google Sheets data, offering a fast management interface with smart pagination and native Dark Mode.

check_circle Operational & Financial Impact

The deployment transformed company dynamics. Digital receipts reach the office seconds after delivery, allowing the finance team to accelerate billing by days. Management gained a macro view of fleet productivity via interactive dashboards, eliminating reliance on manual Excel reports.
